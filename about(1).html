About Us

VISSION

Enriching students minds by providing quality nutritious foods to government schools in Tanzania

MISSION

Ensuring no hunger to students by providing affordable nutritious foods

BACKGROUND DESCRIPTION

The percentage of student who have poor diet in the world is 12.9% according to the statistics done from 2014-2016.by UNFPA.
Almost 66 million of students of primary school in the world attend to school while they are hungry and Africa herself has 23 million students of such kind.
Students in Tanzania stay hungry at school especially in government schools because there's shortage of nutritious food.
also in some school it has been seen there's unsafe food preparation and bad food packaging.
